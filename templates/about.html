Our Story

Chuluni Compassion project was found in 1998 by Jimmy and friends who were driven by love and compassion for young children. It is under christian foundation, where chuluni RGC is the sponsor of the project. Our mission is to give back to society by sponsoring children.

  • We sponsor children from less fortunate families
  • We cater for children's spiritual welfare
  • We equip children with skills
